What I need from you.

You and your team play a big part in developing your website. The site will have 5 pages. Welcome, About Us, Products, Gallery, and Contact Us.

Lets start with the Welcome page and About Us page.

  1. Welcome Page:
    Write short introduction about your company to your potential clients. It's a welcome page. What would you like to say?
  2. About Us Page:
    If anyone wants to quickly understand your brand, your About Us page should be the single link you send them. The best About Us pages accomplish their goals through telling a story about a brand. Start by talking about these things:

    Communicate the story of your business and why you started it.

    Describe the customers or the cause that your business serves.

    Explain your business model.

    Put a face to your business, featuring the founders or the people on your team.
